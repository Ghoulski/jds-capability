Company Overview

JD Service Solutions, doing business as JD's Gleam Team, is a premier provider of custodial, janitorial, and facility cleaning services based in Oklahoma. We deliver reliable, detail-oriented cleaning solutions across residential, post-construction, and light commercial environments.

Our operational flexibility supports multi-site service contracts and recurring facility maintenance schedules. Through trained personnel, defined cleaning protocols, and active supervisory oversight, we ensure all facilities are maintained in a clean, sanitary, and professional condition.

Operating throughout the Tulsa metropolitan area with expansion capabilities, we provide consistent and scalable service delivery for contracts involving multiple facilities or geographically distributed locations.
